Section 10

Practice Problems

Apply what you have learned in each section with focused problem sets. Start from Big-O basics and work your way to advanced dynamic programming.

10.1 How to Use These Problem Sets

  • Try to solve each problem on paper first before coding.
  • Identify the data structure and complexity targets.
  • Then implement in one or more of your preferred languages.
Tip: Timebox yourself (for example, 15-20 minutes per problem). After that, peek at the hint or solution and keep moving.
